FlashRead Sample Passage

This is a short file designed for RSVP testing.
Start at 250 to 320 words per minute.
Then try swipe right to speed up, or tap left to rewind.
You can keep your eyes locked on one focal point,
while each word flashes in place.

Fast reading works best when punctuation adds tiny pauses.
This sentence has commas, periods, and a question mark: can you feel the rhythm?
